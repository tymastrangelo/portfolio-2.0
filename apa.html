What We Do

Hands-on volunteering, low-lift commitments, real impact.

Shelter Volunteering

Support Burlington Humane Society & Animal Services with care tasks, walks, and enrichment.

Dog Day-Outs

Give shelter dogs a break with campus walks and park trips coordinated through the club.

Treat-Baking Workshops

Make pet treats together, learn basic tips, and donate batches to shelters.

Supply Drives

Collect food, toys, and essentials—easy ways to help even on busy weeks.

Campus Events

Pop-ups and awareness campaigns to grow a community that loves animals.

Low-Lift Community

We meet monthly and run weekend events—join as your schedule allows.

FAQ

More questions? Email us.

Do I need prior experience?

Nope—just bring your love for animals. We’ll show you the ropes.

What if I don’t have transportation?

We often coordinate rides to off-campus sites—note it when you sign up.

How often do you meet?

Once a month on campus, plus optional weekend events.

Time commitment?

As much or as little as you’d like. There’s always a way to help.
